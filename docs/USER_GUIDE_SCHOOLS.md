# PerfectMatchSchools - School User Guide

## Welcome to PerfectMatchSchools!

This guide will help you post jobs, find qualified teachers, and manage applications effectively.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the homepage** and click "Sign Up"
2. **Select "School"** as your role
3. **Fill in your information**:
   - School Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (must be at least 8 characters)
4. **Click "Create Account"**
5. You'll be redirected to complete your school profile

### Completing Your School Profile

#### Basic Information
- **School Name**: Your official school name
- **School Type**: Public, Private, Charter, Magnet, International, Online
- **Location**: Full address or city/state
- **Description**: Tell teachers about your school, mission, and values
- **Logo**: Upload your school logo (optional but recommended)

#### Contact Information
- **Email**: Contact email for inquiries
- **Phone**: Contact phone number
- **Website**: School website URL

### Stats Overview

### Navigation

#### Dashboard Tab
View your posted jobs and quick stats.

## Posting Jobs

### Creating a Job Posting

1. **Click "Post Job"** from the sidebar or dashboard
2. **Fill in the job details**:

#### Required Information
- **Job Title**: e.g., "High School Mathematics Teacher"
- **School Name**: Your school name
- **Department**: Mathematics, Science, English/Language Arts, etc.
- **Subject**: Specific subject area
- **Grade Level**: Elementary, Middle School, or High School
- **Job Type**: Full-time, Part-time, or Contract
- **Location**: City, state, or full address
- **Salary Range**: e.g., "$50,000 - $70,000"
- **Job Description**: Detailed description of the position
- **Requirements**: Required qualifications and experience
- **Benefits**: Benefits package details

#### Optional Information
- **Teaching Archetypes**: Select which teaching archetypes would be a good fit:
  - The Guide
  - The Trailblazer
  - The Changemaker
  - The Connector
  - The Explorer
  - The Leader

  Selecting archetypes helps our matching system find teachers who align with your school's teaching philosophy!

3. **Review your posting**
4. **Click "Post Job"**

### Job Posting Best Practices

#### Write Compelling Descriptions
- **Be specific**: Clearly describe the role and responsibilities
- **Highlight benefits**: What makes your school special?
- **Set expectations**: What are you looking for in a candidate?
- **Be authentic**: Show your school's culture and values

#### Set Competitive Salaries
- Research market rates for your area
- Consider total compensation (salary + benefits)
- Be transparent about salary ranges

#### Use Teaching Archetypes
- Select 2-3 archetypes that fit your school culture
- This helps match teachers who align with your values
- Increases quality of applications

### Managing Job Postings

#### View Your Jobs
- See all posted jobs in your dashboard
- View application counts for each job
- See job status (Active/Closed)

#### Edit Jobs
- Update job details if needed
- Modify requirements or descriptions
- Update salary information

#### Close Jobs
- Mark jobs as closed when filled
- Jobs can be reopened if needed

## Finding Candidates

### Automatic Matching

Our matching system automatically finds teachers who:
- Match your selected teaching archetypes
- Have relevant experience and qualifications
- Meet your job requirements

Matched candidates appear in your **Candidates** tab.

### Candidate Dashboard

The Candidates tab shows all applicants organized by job. You can:

#### Filter Candidates
- **By Status**: Applied, Reviewed, Interview, Offer, Rejected
- **By Archetype**: Filter by teaching archetype
- **By Grade Level**: Filter by preferred grade level

#### Search Candidates
- Search by name
- Search by subject
- Search by experience

#### View Modes
- **List View**: Traditional list of candidates
- **Pipeline View**: Kanban-style board by status
- **Compare View**: Side-by-side comparison of selected candidates

### Candidate Profiles

Click on any candidate to view:

#### Profile Information
- Full name and photo
- Bio and teaching philosophy
- Experience and education
- Certifications
- Teaching archetypes

#### Application Details
- Cover letter
- Resume/CV (downloadable)
- Portfolio links
- Application date
- Match score (if applicable)

#### Actions Available
- **View Full Profile**: See complete candidate information
- **Update Status**: Move candidate through hiring stages
- **Add Notes**: Internal notes about the candidate
- **Send Message**: Start a conversation
- **Download Resume**: Save candidate's resume

## Managing Applications

### Application Status Workflow

Move candidates through these stages:

1. **Applied**: Initial application received
2. **Reviewed**: You've reviewed the application
3. **Interview**: Interview scheduled or completed
4. **Offer**: Job offer extended
5. **Rejected**: Not selected for this position

### Updating Candidate Status

1. Select a candidate
2. Click "Update Status"
3. Choose new status
4. Add notes (optional)
5. Save changes

### Bulk Actions

Select multiple candidates to:
- Update status in bulk
- Add notes to multiple candidates
- Export candidate information

### Candidate Comparison

Compare up to 3 candidates side-by-side:
1. Select candidates using checkboxes
2. Click "Compare"
3. View side-by-side comparison
4. Make informed hiring decisions

### Can't post a job?
- Check all required fields are filled

### Not seeing candidates?
- Check your filters - they might be too restrictive

## FAQ

### How does the matching system work?
The matching system uses teaching archetypes, qualifications, and preferences to automatically match teachers to your positions.

### How long should I keep a job posting active?
Postings can stay active as long as needed. Close them when the position is filled.

### Can I edit a job posting after posting?
Yes! You can edit job details at any time.

### How do I know if a candidate viewed my message?
Candidates receive notifications when you message them. Response times vary.

### Can I reject a candidate?
Yes, update their status to "Rejected." They'll receive a notification.

### How many candidates can I compare at once?
You can compare up to 3 candidates side-by-side.

### Can I save candidate information?
Yes, you can download resumes and add notes to candidate profiles.
