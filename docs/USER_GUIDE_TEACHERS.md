# PerfectMatchSchools - Teacher User Guide

## Welcome to PerfectMatchSchools!

This guide will help you navigate the platform and make the most of your job search experience.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the homepage** and click "Sign Up"
2. **Select "Teacher"** as your role
3. **Fill in your information**:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (must be at least 8 characters)
4. **Click "Create Account"**
5. You'll be redirected to complete your profile

### Completing Your Profile

#### Basic Information
- **Bio**: Write a brief introduction about yourself
- **Location**: Where you're located or willing to work
- **Years of Experience**: Your teaching experience
- **Subjects**: Subjects you're qualified to teach
- **Grade Levels**: Grade levels you're comfortable teaching
- **Certifications**: Any teaching certifications you hold

#### Teaching Archetype Quiz
Complete the 8-question quiz to discover your teaching archetype:
- **The Guide**: Structured, organized, takes charge
- **The Trailblazer**: Creative, tech-forward, experimental
- **The Changemaker**: Equity-focused, systemic thinking
- **The Connector**: Team-oriented, consensus-building
- **The Explorer**: Content-deep, standards-focused
- **The Leader**: Takes charge, organizes, establishes clear expectations

Your archetype helps schools find the perfect match for their positions!

#### Resume & Portfolio
- **Upload Resume**: Upload your CV/resume (PDF recommended)
- **Portfolio Links**: Add links to your teaching portfolio, sample lesson plans, or other relevant work

## Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is your command center. Here's what you'll find:

### Stats Overview
- **Applications**: Number of jobs you've applied to
- **Interviews**: Upcoming or scheduled interviews
- **Matches**: Jobs matched to your profile
- **Favorites**: Jobs you've saved
- **Unread Messages**: Messages from schools

### Navigation Tabs

#### Applications Tab
View all your job applications with status updates:
- **Applied**: Your application has been submitted
- **Reviewed**: School has reviewed your application
- **Interview**: Interview scheduled
- **Offer**: Job offer received
- **Rejected**: Application not selected

#### Matched Jobs Tab
See jobs automatically matched to your teaching archetype and preferences. Each match includes:
- Match score (0-100%)
- Why this job matches you
- Quick apply option
- Save for later option

#### Favorites Tab
View all jobs you've saved for later. Perfect for comparing opportunities!

## Finding Jobs

### Browse Jobs Page

Navigate to **"Browse Jobs"** from the sidebar to see all available positions.

#### Search Functionality
- **Search by**: Job title, school name, or location
- **Search suggestions**: See popular searches and your search history

#### Filters
Narrow down your search with filters:
- **Subject**: Mathematics, English, Science, etc.
- **Grade Level**: Elementary, Middle School, High School
- **Job Type**: Full-time, Part-time, Contract
- **Location**: City or region
- **Salary Range**: Set your desired salary range
- **Date Posted**: Find recently posted jobs

#### Sort Options
- **Date Posted (Newest)**: See the latest opportunities first
- **Salary (High to Low)**: Highest paying jobs first
- **Salary (Low to High)**: Entry-level positions first
- **Relevance**: Best matches for you first

#### Pagination
Jobs are displayed 10 per page. Use the pagination controls to navigate through results.

### Job Details

Click on any job to see:
- Full job description
- Requirements and qualifications
- Benefits package
- School information
- Application deadline (if specified)

### Applying to Jobs

#### Quick Apply
1. Click **"Quick Apply"** on a job card
2. Review your resume (from your profile)
3. Write a cover letter
4. Add portfolio links (optional)
5. Click **"Submit Application"**

#### Standard Apply
1. Click on a job to view details
2. Click **"Apply for this Position"**
3. Complete the application form
4. Submit your application

**Tip**: Customize your cover letter for each position to increase your chances!

### Saving Jobs

Click the bookmark icon on any job card to save it for later. Saved jobs appear in your **Favorites** tab.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't log in?
- Check your email and password
- Use "Forgot Password" if needed
- Ensure you're using the correct email

### Not seeing jobs?
- Check your filters - they might be too restrictive
- Try clearing filters and searching again
- Check if you're logged in

### Application not submitting?
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page and submitting again

### Not receiving notifications?
- Check your notification preferences in Settings
- Ensure email notifications are enabled
- Check your spam folder for emails

### Need help?
- Check the FAQ section
- Contact support through the app
- Review this guide for common questions

## FAQ

### How does the matching system work?
The matching system uses your teaching archetype, preferences, and qualifications to find jobs that align with your teaching style and experience.

### Can I apply to multiple jobs?
Yes! You can apply to as many jobs as you'd like.

### How long does it take to hear back?
Response times vary by school. Some respond within days, others may take weeks.

### Can I edit my application after submitting?
Once submitted, applications typically cannot be edited. Contact the school directly if you need to update information.

### How do I know if a school viewed my application?
You'll receive a notification when your application status changes to "Reviewed."

### Can I save jobs without applying?
Yes! Use the bookmark icon to save jobs for later.

### How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Account Management → Delete Account. This action is permanent.
